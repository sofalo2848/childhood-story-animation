The Making of "In the Eyes of Then"

This project was born from a desire to capture the magical feeling of Saturday morning cartoons. We wanted to create an interactive experience that would transport viewers back to their childhood.

The process involved recording authentic audio, creating nostalgic visuals, and developing an intuitive interface that would make the experience feel natural and immersive.
